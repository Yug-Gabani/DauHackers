Direct Impact on Coastal Communities

How our system benefits different user groups

Shopkeepers & Businesses

  • Prepare for floods/erosion and storm surges
  • Protect inventory and infrastructure
  • Plan business operations around weather
  • Reduce economic losses

Fisherfolk

  • SMS alerts without smartphones
  • Avoid dangerous fishing conditions
  • Save boats, nets, and lives
  • Plan safe fishing trips

Tourism Industry

  • Warn customers about unsafe beaches
  • Reduce accidents and liabilities
  • Plan activities around conditions
  • Maintain safety standards

Families & Beachgoers

  • Live safety status (Green/Red)
  • Check conditions before swimming
  • Early evacuation information
  • Peace of mind during visits

Everyday Relevance, Not Just Disaster-Time

Supporting daily activities and long-term planning

Daily Planning

Daily tide & weather forecasts help fishermen plan safe fishing trips and beachgoers check real-time conditions before swimming.

Long-term Monitoring

NGOs monitor slow changes like erosion and sea level rise for long-term planning and policy development.

Sustainable Living

Show data on coastal erosion & pollution trends to make locals aware of how daily actions affect the coast.

Preventive Action

Authorities can stop illegal sand mining or dumping before damage spreads to protect coastal habitats.
